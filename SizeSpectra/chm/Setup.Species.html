Species Setup Function

Description

Produces a list of species specific constants in a standardised format.

Usage

Setup.Species(alpha, beta, lambda, A, K, mu_0, mu_s, epsilon, u_0, sig, q_0, prey, pred, comp, gamma_prey, gamma_pred, gamma_comp, speciesname)

Arguments

alpha The volume search allometric exponent
beta The intrinsic mortality allometric exponent
lambda The slope of the default plankton size spectrum
A The volume search coefficient
K The energy conversion efficiency
mu_0 The intrinsic mortality coefficient
mu_s The senescence mortality coefficient
epsilon The senescence mortality 'sharpness' coefficient
u_0 The intercept of the default plankton size spectrum
sig The feeding mass ratio kernel standard deviation
q_0 The feeding mass ratio kernel mean
prey The prey seeking spatial movement coefficient
pred The predator avoiding spatial movement coefficient
comp The competition avoiding spatial movement coefficient
gamma_prey The prey seeking spatial movement allometric exponent
gamma_pred The predator avoiding spatial movement allometric exponent
gamma_comp The competition avoiding spatial movement allometric exponent
speciesname The name of the species. This must be unique if more than one species is under investigation as it used to construct the output results text files.
